About Us

DrukHeirloom

"Druk Heirloom" likely refers to items passed down through generations in Bhutan, often associated with the country's rich cultural heritage and traditions, particularly textiles. The term "Druk" is synonymous with Bhutan, meaning "Land of the Thunder Dragon". These heirlooms can include textiles like yathra, a unique weave from central Bhutan, and other craft items. Heirlooms are items of significant value, often passed down within families for their sentimental or historical importance.

Mission

  • Preserve and Celebrate:Craft authentic Bhutanese heirloom products—like kaya raidas, textiles, and art—using traditional techniques passed down through generations.
  • Educate and Connect:Share the deep meanings behind each heirloom piece with customers through storytelling, workshops, and digital media—building cultural awareness and appreciation globally.
    • Commit to Sustainability: Use eco-friendly materials, ethical production methods, and responsible packaging to honor Bhutan's ethos of harmony with nature.

Vision

  • Preservation of CultureTo safeguard and revive endangered Bhutanese art forms, textiles, and oral traditions.
    • Global RecognitionTo become a globally respected name that symbolizes authenticity, spirituality, and elegance rooted in Bhutanese culture.
  • Cultural PrideTo inspire Bhutanese youth to value their heritage and see cultural craftsmanship as a path of dignity and innovation.
