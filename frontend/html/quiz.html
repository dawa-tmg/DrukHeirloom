DrukHeirloom

Druk Heirloom is a digital tribute to Bhutan's rich cultural and artistic heritage. Rooted in the nation's deep reverence for tradition, this project seeks to preserve and promote the unique visual identity of Bhutan through typography. Inspired by ancient scripts, religious manuscripts, and the intricate calligraphy found in dzongs and monasteries, Druk Heirloom reflects the elegance and harmony of Bhutanese design. Whether used in educational materials, digital projects, or cultural displays, it helps connect modern audiences with the timeless essence of Bhutanese identity. More than just a font, Druk Heirloom is a symbolic bridge between Bhutan's spiritual past and its creative future.
